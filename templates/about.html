About WrestleRate

The Motivation

As I've watched wrestling for the past 10+ years, I've loved to rank/rate various matches and decide which ones I like "best". However, I feel there was no definitive site where I could store this ratings and view the distribution of them. That's why I created WrestleRate.

One of my main focuses of this web app was a individualistic focus rather than the community type ratings you see on other sites. I simply wanted an app where I and others could view our own ratings without getting influenced by the general opinion. I also believe that this more freethinking approach discourages tribalism, which I'm not a fan of. Because of this, I have no plans to implement a "community" match score system in the near feature however comments are a possibility.

Thank you for visiting, and I look forward to sharing more with you!

How to Use

The interface is pretty self-explanatory. You login or create your account and just start rating wrestling matches that you have seen. Your distribution of ratings in the bottom will be updated after every input. If you want to delete a match, simply click the red X next to it. If you want to see your average rating by superstar, click the "See By Superstar" button.
